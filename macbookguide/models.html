Models

Have you ever been at an Apple Store or at your local BestBuy, stand in front of the table where all computers are and randomly start looking at the price tags without having any idea of what to choose or what to do? I have, and that is why I want to help a little, so you can go to your nearest store and be able to know what are the different models and their features. First let me show you a list of all macbook models and a few pictures of each, to help you recognize how do they look like and what they offer. - Macbook Air 13.3” - Air 13.6” - Pro 14 and 16” - Pro 13.3”. Now that you know the difference on processors and what Macbook Models include them, let’s take a look at some features that can be beneficial for our intended use on the computer. Screen quality and size - Fingerprint Reader - Battery Life - Ports - Weight - Touchbar - Type of charger - Camera quality - Storage space - RAM Memory

Macbook Air M1

This computer is a 2020 Model with a M1 processor. It comes with a starting 8gb of RAM, 256gb of storage (SSD) and it's main characterisic is being light. Perfect for high-school students or personal use.

Macbook Air M2

This computer is a 2022 Model with a M2 processor. It comes with a starting 8gb of RAM, 256gb of storage (SSD). The storage can be customized up to 512gb. It comes with a 13.6" retina display, which makes it slightly bigger than its predecessor. It also has a touch ID button that allows you to unlock your computer with your fingerprint, process payments and download apps. Perfect for college students, personal use, photo editing, programming, etc.

Macbook Pro M1

This computer is a 2020 Model with a M1 processor. It comes with a standart 8gb of RAM, 256gb of storage (SSD). The storage can be customized up to 512gb. The screen size is 13.3" and comes with a touchbar that can recognize your activity on the computer and suggest commands and buttons as you use it.

Macbook Pro M2

This computer is a 2022 Model with a M2 processor. It comes with a starting 8gb of RAM, 256gb of storage (SSD). The storage can be customized up to 512gb. It comes with a 13.3" display (Same as the 2020 Model). It comes with a touchbar that can recognize your activity on the computer and suggest commands and buttons as you use it (Same as the 2020 Model). Perfect for college students, personal use, photo editing, programming, etc. A better performance is experienced thanks to it's ventilated fans which helps the computer not to overheat.

Macbook Pro M1 Pro 14"

This computer is a 2022 Model with a M1 Pro processor. It comes with a starting 16gb of RAM, 512gb of storage (SSD). The storage can be customized up to 2TB. It comes with a 14" retina display, which makes it slightly bigger than the M1 or M2 model. It also has a touch ID button that allows you to unlock your computer with your fingerprint, process payments and download apps. Perfect for college students whose careers require a powerful computer, photo editing, videography, casual gaming, programming, accounting, etc. It is the faster computer built by the company so far.

Macbook Pro M1 Pro 16"

This computer is a 2022 Model with a M1 Pro processor. It comes with a starting 16gb of RAM, 512gb of storage (SSD). The storage can be customized up to 2TB. It comes with a 16" retina display, which makes it significantly bigger than the 14" version. It also has a touch ID button that allows you to unlock your computer with your fingerprint, process payments and download apps. Perfect for college students whose careers require a powerful computer, photo editing, videography, casual gaming, programming, accounting, etc. It is the faster computer built by the company so far. Only difference between this and the smaller version is the size of the screen, better speakers and better battery life
